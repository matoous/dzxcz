European Union is working on [EU Digital Identity Wallet](https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/display/EUDIGITALIDENTITYWALLET), a digital ID and personal digital wallet for EU citizens, residents and businesses.

Should become widely available in 2026.

It's a continuation of [eIDAS](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/eidas-regulation) from 2014.

On 29 February 2024, the European Parliament adopted the proposed regulation (Regulation (EU) 2024/1183) with 335 votes to 190, and 31 abstentions. It entered into force on 20 May following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
